Welcome to The Film Team (Sri Lanka)

THE FILM TEAM is Sri Lanka’s leading FILM & TV PRODUCTION Service Company. We Specialize in Feature Films, Commercials, Television Drama, Reality Shows, Documentaries & Music Videos. We are a highly professional one-stop service provider for producers and agencies shooting anywhere in Sri Lanka

Why Film in Sri Lanka?

  • Film friendly country
  • The whole island is a film set.
  • A variety of unique locations close to each other
  • Two international airports servicing leading airlines in the world.
  • Non-Union Crew
  • Speedy approval of permits & permissions
  • The availability of Caucasian and Asian extras
  • Zero rating for value added tax.
  • Lowest crew & equipment rates in the region

Why Film with The Film Team?

  • Years of experience in handling international productions in the region
  • Professional and reliable production & technical crew
  • Talented and artistic set design & construction team
  • Maintains a cordial business relationships with all film related governmental establishments
  • Modern filming & support equipment at economical rates
  • We show you the land like no other.
  • Realistic and detailed “below the line” Budgets based on very competitive rates.
